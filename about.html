What Fargo Refresh Is

Fargo Refresh is a local pressure washing service focused on residential exterior cleaning throughout Fargo, Moorhead, West Fargo, and nearby surrounding communities.

Most jobs involve concrete, siding, patios, decks, fences, and outdoor surfaces that gradually collect dirt, algae, weather staining, and buildup over time.

The goal is simple: show up on time, communicate clearly, do solid work, and leave surfaces looking noticeably cleaner than before.
