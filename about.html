What This Is

Fargo Fresh is a small local pressure washing service operating in the Fargo–Moorhead area. We focus on residential exterior cleaning and keep the process simple from start to finish.

Most of the work is driveways, siding, decks, and patios. If it’s outside and built up with dirt, algae, or weather staining, that’s what we handle.
