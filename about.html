What We Do

Fargo Fresh is a small local pressure washing service focused on residential exterior cleaning throughout the Fargo–Moorhead area.

Most jobs involve driveways, siding, patios, decks, and outdoor surfaces that collect buildup from weather, dirt, algae, and everyday use.

The goal is simple: show up, do solid work, communicate clearly, and leave the property looking noticeably cleaner than when we arrived.
